Rather than relying on hours of effort to hand-create groups, sorting hat takes into account every variable given by the students to find the optimal group for each student and sponsor in about a minute.

Finding the optimal group for everyone

After all the survey results are collected, sorting hat takes each student's preferences into account with measures of importance determined by the instructor, and returns a final list of projects and the students most suited to work on them together.
